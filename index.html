What Is IyaSchool?

IyaSchool is a school management platform. It provides a convenient and efficient way to maintain school records as well as manage your day to day administration. IyaSchool platform is accessible via your desktop, your tablet or your mobile phone.

Key Benefits

1. Accessive via web and mobile
2. Secure – jobtitle based access rights
3. Guest user access to general info
4. Robust search functionality
5. A cost effective solution
6. Continual features enhancements
